Welcome to Fork and Function!

This is your go-to destination for delicious, nutritious recipes and a fun, little engaging nutrition game! A CMP project consisted of members Olivia Beom, Yoonie Chae and Abbie Pont, we have curated and searched for healthy recipes to help you explore healthy eating habits while having fun. We hope you find this site useful during the next exam season!

Explore our Recipes section for a variety of healthy meal ideas, and don't forget to check out our Game for a fun way to relax during study breaks or while waiting for your oven to finish baking your goods.

Why Does Healthy Eating Matter?

Healthy eating is essential for maintaining your overall well-being, and benefits your studying!

  • Boosting energy levels
  • Improving mood and mental health
  • Supporting immune function
  • Promoting healthy growth and development
  • Reducing the risk of chronic diseases
  • Enhancing cognitive function
  • Improving concentration and focus
