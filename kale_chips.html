Kale Chips

Source of recipe: https://www.eatthis.com/how-to-make-kale-chips/

Allergens

  • Gluten free
  • Dairy free
  • Nut free

Ingredients

  • Kale
  • 10mL of olive oil
  • Salt

Optional Ingredients

  • Pinch of chilli powder or paprika to season the kale prior to baking

Tips

  • Do not overcrowd the baking sheets. That will get you steamed kale, and it won’t crisp.
  • Turn the pans around and switch them from top to bottom at least once during baking time.
  • Remove the chips as they’re done; some might take longer than others.

Instructions

  1. Prep the kale by removing any thick stems and tearing up the leaves. Make sure if you wash the kale (if it isn't pre-washed) that you dry it thoroughly.
  2. Toss it in a bowl with a little bit of olive oil. You want just enough to lightly coat the kale—don't saturate it, and season lightly with salt and any other seasonings you want.
  3. Spread the kale out on baking sheets and bake at 120ºC until the leaves are golden and crisp. They'll crisp up more as they cool.
  4. Cook between 20 and 30 minutes.
  5. Enjoy!
