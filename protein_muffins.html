Protein Muffins

Source of recipe: https://mattsfitchef.com/protein-muffins/#wprm-recipe-container-16444

Allergens

  • Contains dairy

Ingredients

  • 1 large egg
  • ⅔ cup (180 g) Greek yogurt
  • 4 oz (115 g) ripe banana
  • ⅓ cup (80 ml) maple syrup, or honey
  • 1 ½ cups (150 g) flour (wheat)
  • ½ cup (50 g) protein powder, see notes for recommendations
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • ⅙ tsp salt
  • 3 to 6 tbsp milk of choice, see notes
  • ¼ cup (50g) chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C and prepare a muffin mold with muffin liners. Make sure you grease the liners to prevent the cupcakes from sticking.
  2. Stir in shredded coconut and chocolate chips.
  3. Roll mixture into small balls and place on a baking sheet.
  4. Chill in the refrigerator for 30 minutes before serving.
  5. Enjoy!
