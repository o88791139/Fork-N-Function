Trail Mix

Source of recipe: https://www.thehealthymaven.com/how-to-build-a-healthy-trail-mix/

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups raw nuts (almonds, pecans, cashews, peanuts etc..)
  • 1 cup raw seeds (sunflower seeds, pumpkin seeds etc…)
  • 1 cup unsweetened, unsulphured dried fruit
  • 1/4 tsp sea salt
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  • Pinch of nutmeg

Optional Ingredients

  • Fun stuff (1/2 cup chopped dark chocolate, 1 cup popped popcorn, 1 cup pretzels etc...)

Instructions

  1. Combine all ingredients in a large bowl and mix well.
  2. Store in a ziploc bag or mason jar
  3. Enjoy!
