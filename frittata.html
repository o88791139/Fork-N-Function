Frittata

Source of recipe: Family recipe

Allergens

  • Gluten free
  • Contains dairy

Ingredients

  • 2 large zucchinis
  • 2 carrots
  • 1 cup of grated cheese
  • 2 cups chickpea flour
  • 3/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 5 eggs
  • 1/2 cup feta crumbled into chunks
  • Salt & pepper

Instructions

  1. Grate zucchini, carrots and cheese
  2. Combine with flour, oil and lightly beaten eggs with a dash of salt and pepper
  3. Pour into a well greased 16x26cm tin
  4. Bake in moderate oven for 30-40 minutes - ensure middle is cooked through
  5. Enjoy!

Note: add more flour if moisture is too wet
